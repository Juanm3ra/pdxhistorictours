Site Goals
The customers wants the website to bring new clients to the business, they want the customers to be able contact them through the website. In addition, they want the customers to feel like they hare visiting a professional touring page.
Basic demographic information
People who are interested in learning about portland, those who want to learn about Portland will visit the site. It could be someone already in portland or someone planning to visit.
Deeper dive into the site goal
An opportunity the client wants to address is that they have more years of experience and they provide a more complete tour experience. They want to address the marketing, and searching terms.
Call(s) to action
The client wants the visitors to call, fill out contact form, search for information, and book a tour.
Competitive analysis
Two related websites: http://oregontourco.com/ https://www.seatosummit.net/ They are related because they are both touring websites for Portland, Oregon. The dropdown menus are nice. The colors of the first website are great, they go with the state of Oregon. Probably add a couple of videos on the clients website; everyone likes videos, what better way to advertise.
